Dashboard:is a user-friendly interface designed for personal investors to monitor and manage their investment portfolio. It includes several sections.
The main overview, including quick access to the portfolio's total value and the user's last login information.
Detailed information about the user's accounts, including balances and rates of return.
A graphical representation of the account's performance over time, including investment returns.
A visual and numerical representation of the distribution of assets between different categories, such as stocks and bonds, along with the targets for each category.
A log of the latest activities within the account, specifically dividend receipts in this case.
